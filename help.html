About This Website

This website is designed to make reading educational notes and PDFs easier for visually impaired users. It uses text-to-speech (TTS) and voice commands to help you read documents without needing to rely only on vision.

Themes & Accessibility

You can Increase/Decrease Font-Size of Text.

You can change Theme(Light/Dark) depending upon your choice.

Apply there features by clicking on Settings button.

Uploading Notes

Click on Choose File option.

Choose a PDF file from your computer.

Apply there features by clicking on Settings button.

Reading Aloud

Press Read Aloud to start listening to your uploaded notes.

Press Stop button to stop reading.

You can access these options by clicking on Setting buttons.

Voice Commands

You can also control website using your voice.

First click Enable Voice button.

Then follow instructions written on Popup menu.
